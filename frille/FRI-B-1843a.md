:::{admonition} Explanation of terms

- **Einlieger** = a lodger/sub-tenant or cottager **without his own farm or land**. It’s a residential/tenure status
(where/how someone lives). Einlieger appears to be abbreviated as **Ein** in the image.
- **Taglöhner** (Tagelöhner) = a day laborer, hired as needed and paid by the day. It’s an **occupation/economic role**.
